Diet Plan

💊 Supplements: EAA (pre-workout), Whey+Creatine (post-workout), Fish Oil+Multivitamin (breakfast).
🥛 Protein: Paneer, Soya, Peanut Butter, Oats spread daily.
💧 Hydration: 3–4L water daily.
😴 Sleep: 7–8 hours minimum.

6-Day Workout Split

MON – Chest + Triceps

  • Push-ups: 3×20–30
  • Flat Bench Press: 4×6–8
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3×8–10
  • Pec Deck: 3×12–15
  • Bar Dips: 3×failure
  • Triceps Pushdown: 3×10–12
  • Overhead Triceps Extension: 3×10–12
  • ABS: Hanging Leg Raise 3×15

TUE – Back + Biceps

  • Pull-ups: 4×max
  • Lat Pulldown: 3×10–12
  • Barbell Row: 3×8–10
  • Seated Cable Row: 3×12
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curl: 3×10–12
  • Hammer Curl: 3×10–12
  • Face Pulls: 3×12–15
  • ABS: Plank Hold 3×60s

WED – Legs

  • Squats: 4×6–8
  • Leg Press: 3×10–12
  • Walking Lunges: 3×12 each leg
  • Leg Extension: 3×12–15
  • Standing Calf Raises: 4×15–20
  • ABS: Cable Crunch 3×15

THU – Shoulders + Traps

  • Overhead Press: 4×8–10
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises: 3×12–15
  • Arnold Press: 3×10–12
  • Rear Delt Fly: 3×12–15
  • Shrugs: 3×12–15
  • ABS: Russian Twists 3×20

FRI – Back + Arms

  • Deadlift: 4×5–6
  • Chest Supported Row: 3×8–10
  • Wide Grip Pulldown: 3×10–12
  • Rear Delt Fly: 3×12
  • Preacher Curl: 3×10–12
  • Reverse Curl: 3×10–12
  • ABS: Hanging Knee Raise 3×15

SAT – Hamstrings + Glutes

  • Romanian Deadlift: 4×8–10
  • Leg Curl: 4×10–12
  • Hip Thrusts: 4×10–12
  • Bulgarian Split Squat: 3×12 each leg
  • Seated Calf Raises: 4×15–20
  • ABS: Plank Variations 3×30s

SUN – Rest / Active Recovery
