Goal: Lean-Muscular • 16y

Diet Plan

Protein target: 1.6–2 g/kg Water: 3–4 L/day Sleep: 7–8 hrs

Pre-Workout (4:50–5:00 am)

  • EAA – 1 scoop (water)
  • 1 banana or 2 dates

Post-Workout (6:30 am)

  • Whey – 1 scoop + Creatine 3–5 g
  • Fast carbs: banana / papaya / watermelon
  • 5 soaked almonds

Breakfast (7:30–8:00 am)

  • Oats (40–50 g) + milk + 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • Paneer (50–80 g) or 2 boiled eggs
  • Multivitamin + Fish oil

Lunch (12–1 pm)

  • Roti (2–3) or Rice (150–200 g cooked)
  • Dal + Sabzi
  • Protein: Paneer / Soya / Chana (~30 g)
  • Salad

Evening Snack (4–5 pm)

  • Peanut butter sandwich (2 bread + 1 tbsp)
  • or Sprouted moong / Roasted chana

Dinner (8–9 pm)

  • Roti/Rice + Paneer/Soya/Dal + Sabzi
  • Salad

Before Bed (10–10:30 pm)

  • Warm milk (200 ml) + 2 soaked almonds
  • Optional: 50 g paneer (extra protein)

Workout Plan (6-Day, Baki-style)

Compounds 6–10 reps • Isolation 10–15 reps • Core 15–20 reps • Progressive overload + perfect form.

MON – Chest + Triceps (Push)

  • Push-ups (warm-up) — 3×20–30
  • Flat Bench Press — 4×6–8
  • Incline Dumbbell Press — 3×8–10
  • Pec Deck / Cable Fly — 3×12–15
  • Bar Dips — 3×failure
  • Triceps Pushdown — 3×10–12
  • Overhead Triceps Extension — 3×10–12
  • Abs: Hanging Leg Raise — 3×15

TUE – Back + Biceps (Pull)

  • Pull-ups — 4×max
  • Lat Pulldown — 3×10–12
  • Barbell Row — 3×8–10
  • Seated Cable Row — 3×12
  • Dumbbell Curl — 3×10–12
  • Hammer Curl — 3×10–12
  • Face Pulls — 3×12–15
  • Abs: Plank — 3×60s

WED – Legs (Quads focus)

  • Back Squat — 4×6–8
  • Leg Press — 3×10–12
  • Walking Lunges — 3×12/leg
  • Leg Extension — 3×12–15
  • Standing Calf Raises — 4×15–20
  • Abs: Cable Crunch — 3×15

THU – Shoulders + Traps

  • Overhead Press (BB/DB) — 4×8–10
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises — 3×12–15
  • Arnold Press — 3×10–12
  • Rear Delt Fly — 3×12–15
  • Shrugs — 3×12–15
  • Abs: Russian Twists — 3×20

FRI – Back + Arms (Strength)

  • Deadlift — 4×5–6
  • Chest Supported Row — 3×8–10
  • Wide Grip Pulldown — 3×10–12
  • Rear Delt Fly — 3×12
  • Preacher Curl — 3×10–12
  • Reverse Curl — 3×10–12
  • Abs: Hanging Knee Raise — 3×15

SAT – Hamstrings + Glutes

  • Romanian Deadlift — 4×8–10
  • Leg Curl (Machine) — 4×10–12
  • Hip Thrusts — 4×10–12
  • Bulgarian Split Squat — 3×12/leg
  • Seated Calf Raises — 4×15–20
  • Abs: Plank variations — 3×30s
Sun: Rest / Active Recovery — walk, stretch, mobility, foam roll (optional 20–30 min easy cardio)
