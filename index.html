Baki Winter Diet — Eggetarian Plan (Gym 5:00–7:00 PM)

Morning (7:00–7:30 AM)

  • Warm water + lemon + 5 soaked almonds + 1 walnut
  • Optional: 1 tsp chyawanprash or honey (for winter immunity)

Breakfast (8:00–8:30 AM)

  • Oats 50 g (cooked) + 1 banana + 1 tbsp peanut butter + 1 scoop pea protein (mix with warm milk)
  • Or: Paneer bhurji (100 g) + 2 rotis

Mid-Morning Snack (11:00 AM)

  • 2 boiled eggs
  • Or roasted chana / fruit (apple/orange)

Lunch (1:30–2:00 PM)

  • 2 rotis + 1 small bowl rice
  • Dal / Rajma / Chole + paneer or tofu (protein source)
  • Cooked veggies + salad + 1 tsp ghee

Pre-Workout (4:15–4:30 PM)

  • 1 banana + 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • Black coffee (optional) — take 30–45 minutes before workout

Post-Workout (7:00–7:20 PM)

  • 1 scoop Pea Protein (unflavoured) mixed in water or warm milk
  • Creatine 3–5 g (mix with post-workout shake) — daily
  • Quick carbs if needed: banana or slice brown bread

Dinner (8:30–9:00 PM)

  • 2 rotis + paneer/tofu / dal + cooked vegetables
  • Optional: 1 glass warm milk before bed

Before Sleep (10:00–10:30 PM)

  • Curd / Greek yogurt 50 g OR warm milk with a pinch of turmeric
  • Hydration target: 3–3.5 L/day (even in winter)

Supplements & Quick Notes

• Multivitamin (HK Vitals): take with breakfast every morning.
• Fish oil (HK Vitals): with lunch (daily).
• Creatine: 3–5 g daily — mix with post-workout protein shake (or any drink).
• EAA: sip during workout (intra-workout) or as pre-workout if needed.
• Pea Protein: post-workout and as needed to hit protein target (~1.6–2 g/kg/day).
• Peanut butter & oats: great daily fats & carbs — include in breakfast or pre-workout.
• Protein target: aim ~110–140 g/day for your weight & goals. Sleep 7–8 hrs.

Transformation Workout — Winter Edition (3 Phases)

This plan is structured as Phase 1 → Phase 2 → Phase 3 (6–9 month roadmap). You train 6 days/week. Focus on progressive overload and clean form.

Phase 1 — Foundation (Dec–Feb)

Structure: 6 days (Push / Pull / Legs) — moderate volume, learn tempo and control.

MON – Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
- Barbell Bench Press 4×6–8
- Incline Dumbbell Press 3×8–10
- Overhead Press 3×8–10
- Dumbbell Lateral Raise 3×12–15
- Triceps Pushdown 3×10–12
- Hanging Leg Raise 3×12–15

TUE – Pull (Back, Biceps)
- Pull-ups 4×max
- Barbell Row 3×6–8
- Seated Cable Row 3×10–12
- Face Pulls 3×12–15
- Dumbbell Curls 3×10–12
- Plank 3×60s

WED – Legs (Quads focus)
- Back Squat 4×6–8
- Romanian Deadlift 3×8–10
- Leg Press 3×10–12
- Walking Lunges 3×12 each
- Standing Calf Raise 4×15–20

THU – Push (variation, slightly lighter)
- Incline DB Press 4×8–10
- Cable Fly 3×12–15
- Arnold Press 3×10–12
- Triceps Dips 3×failure
- Core: Cable Crunch 3×15

FRI – Pull (strength focus)
- Deadlift 4×4–6
- Wide-Grip Lat Pulldown 3×10
- Chest-Supported Row 3×8–10
- Hammer Curls 3×10–12
- Rear Delt Fly 3×12–15

SAT – Legs (Hamstrings & Glutes)
- Romanian Deadlift 4×8–10
- Hip Thrust 4×8–10
- Bulgarian Split Squat 3×12 each
- Seated Calf Raise 4×15
- Mobility & light core work

SUN – Rest / Active recovery

Phase 2 — Growth & Volume (Mar–May)

Structure: Increase weekly volume & intensity. Add 1–2 intensity techniques (drop sets, short rest supersets).
- Train same split but slightly higher reps (8–12) and occasional supersets.
- Add 10–20% calories (clean surplus) and keep protein high.
- Continue creatine + EAA; track weekly measurements.

Phase 3 — Definition & Refinement (Jun–Aug)

Structure: Preserve muscle, reduce body fat, increase conditioning.
- Slight calorie reduction, add 2 cardio sessions/week (15–25 min incline walk / HIIT).
- Keep strength training 4–5x/week, focus on time under tension and posture.
- Maintain protein, prioritize sleep & recovery.

Transformation Notes — Supplements, Recovery & Rules

• Supplement timing: Multivitamin after breakfast; Fish oil with lunch; Creatine with post-workout shake; Pea protein immediately post-workout; EAA during workout as needed.
• Sleep & Recovery: 7–8 hours nightly. If tired, reduce volume or take an extra rest day — do not skip protein or creatine.
• Tracking: Take progress photos every 2 weeks, note weights/reps, and measure arms, chest, waist monthly.
• Diet rule: 90% clean food, 1 weekly cheat allowed. Aim 1.6–2 g protein/kg bodyweight. Hydrate 3–3.5 L/day.
• Mindset: be consistent for 6–9 months. Progress is cumulative — small weekly gains add up.
