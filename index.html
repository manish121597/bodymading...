Diet Plan

Pre-Workout (4:50–5:00am)

  • EAA — 1 scoop with water
  • 1 banana or 2 dates (quick fuel)

Post-Workout (6:30am)

  • Whey — 1 scoop + 3–5g Creatine
  • Fast carbs: banana / papaya / watermelon
  • 5 soaked almonds

Breakfast (7:30–8:00am)

  • Oats (40–50g) + milk + 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • Paneer (50–80g) or 2 boiled eggs
  • Multivitamin + Fish oil

Lunch (12–1pm)

  • Roti (2–3) or Rice (150–200g cooked)
  • Dal + Sabzi
  • Paneer / Soya / Chana (~30g protein)
  • Salad

Evening Snack (4–5pm)

  • Peanut butter sandwich (2 slices + 1 tbsp PB)
  • or Sprouted moong / Roasted chana

Dinner (8–9pm)

  • Roti / Rice + Paneer / Soya / Dal + Sabzi
  • Salad

Supplements & Notes

• EAA: pre-workout (you said intra not possible, so pre is best).
• Whey + Creatine: post-workout. Creatine 3–5g daily.
• Fish oil + Multivitamin: with breakfast.
• Daily protein target: 1.6–2 g/kg bodyweight. Hydration: 3–4 L/day. Sleep: 7–8 hrs.

Workout Plan (6-Day Split)

MON – Chest + Triceps (Push)
- Push-ups 3×20–30
- Flat Bench Press 4×6–8
- Incline Dumbbell Press 3×8–10
- Pec Deck / Cable Fly 3×12–15
- Bar Dips 3×failure
- Triceps Pushdown 3×10–12
- Overhead Triceps Extension 3×10–12
- ABS: Hanging Leg Raise 3×15

TUE – Back + Biceps (Pull)
- Pull-ups 4×max
- Lat Pulldown 3×10–12
- Barbell Row 3×8–10
- Seated Cable Row 3×12
- Dumbbell Bicep Curl 3×10–12
- Hammer Curl 3×10–12
- Face Pulls 3×12–15
- ABS: Plank 3×60s

WED – Legs (Quads focus)
- Squats 4×6–8
- Leg Press 3×10–12
- Walking Lunges 3×12 each
- Leg Extension 3×12–15
- Standing Calf Raises 4×15–20
- ABS: Cable Crunch 3×15

THU – Shoulders + Traps
- Overhead Press (BB/DB) 4×8–10
- Dumbbell Lateral Raises 3×12–15
- Arnold Press 3×10–12
- Rear Delt Fly 3×12–15
- Shrugs 3×12–15
- ABS: Russian Twists 3×20

FRI – Back + Arms (Pull/Strength)
- Deadlift 4×5–6
- Chest Supported Row 3×8–10
- Wide Grip Pulldown 3×10–12
- Rear Delt Fly 3×12
- Preacher Curl 3×10–12
- Reverse Curl 3×10–12
- ABS: Hanging Knee Raise 3×15

SAT – Hamstrings + Glutes
- Romanian Deadlift 4×8–10
- Leg Curl (Machine) 4×10–12
- Hip Thrusts 4×10–12
- Bulgarian Split Squat 3×12 each leg
- Seated Calf Raises 4×15–20
- ABS: Plank Variations 3×30s

SUN – Rest / Active recovery (walking, stretching, foam rolling)
